Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the OCR and Translation Tool?

OCR is basically a technology that converts different types of documents, such as scanned documents, PDF, and images captured. It recognizes text characters in images and translating them into a machine-readable text.

2. What could be the possible limitations of the OCR and Translation Tool?

The possible limitations of the OCR and Translation Tool are basically its Accuracy Issues; sometimes there will be inaccurate translation of some words. Language Support and Processing Time; some OCRs may or may not support different languages while the processing time is just basically the waiting time of the user.

3. Is the OCR and Translation Tool for free?

This OCR and Translation Tool is designed and for free of use for users in the community.

4. What are the languages used for translation?

There are only 3 current languages that are presently used in the OCR and Translation Tool: English, Spanish, and French.
